University of Toronto: Course Scheduler


Are you trying to figure out your course plans before ROSI opens? Wasting lots of time with Google calendar and/or Excel? This UofT course scheduler allows you to preview your timetable for courses in Engineering or Arts and Science* at the St. George campus.

To register a new account or log back into the service, enter your username (Minimum of 4 characters, alphanumeric only):

Your username:
Benefits:
- Displays Fall and Winter timetables in convenient calendar format
- Enables easy adding/deleting of courses and tutorials
- (Engineers only) Identifies Humanities electives
- Automatically generates a timetable with the fewest possible section conflicts given a series of courses (beta feature!)
Important Note: All timetables created before July 30 2013 had to be removed in order to comply with Faculty regulations concerning privacy and security, as those timetables were created using UTORids as the usernames. I apologize for any inconvenience. Please do NOT use your UTORid as a username when using this tool.
*Not all ArtSci courses may be in the system. This is due to inconsistent formatting of the official ArtSci timetable listings. Please contact me for any concerns.
